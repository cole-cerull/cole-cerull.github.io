BCycle
Consulting Report

As a project for our Technical Design and Communications class, our team of three constructed a Consulting report for our stakeholder, the manager of BCycle in Boulder. BCycle is bike share program that operates in boulder and caters to student with their electric bike stations located around campus and town. The use of the bikes is free to students at CU. Our stakeholder reached out in hopes of developing strategies for increasing ridership, especially among students who had never used the service. Our team
