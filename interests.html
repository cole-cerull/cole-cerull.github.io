interests

I have always been interested in many different hobbies and activites from 3d printing to welding and fabrication. Growing up in a family of hobby fabricatiors and woodworkers, I always had mentorship and access to experiment in the workshop. My parents somehow put up with my shenanigans of building flamethrowers in the basement and taking over the garage with projects and my car which spent more of its life with the hood up than down. While at Tesla Engineering Charter school I was intorduced to electronics, CAD modeling, welding, machining and even getting certified to run quarter million dollar CNC machines. Through my life I would find something interesting and then devote my time to learn everything I could about that wether it be hydroponic gardening or how to repurpose a beer can for shimming the backlash in the transmission I was rebuilding.
